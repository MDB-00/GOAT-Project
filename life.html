Connor Kenway

The GOAT of the Assassin's Creed video game series.

Early Life

Born with the name of Ratonhnhaké:ton, Connor was born to his mother, Kaniehtí:io, and father, a british man named Hatham Kenway. He was raised in a Mohawk village and at age 4 he and a group of friends were playing a game of hide and seek, it was there when he encounted members of the Templar Order. He was then knocked unconscious and awoke to the sight of his village in flames.

Young Connor was unable to save his mother who was trapped in their burning home. Since then, Connor vowed to bring down the Templar Order and protect his people from further harm.
