Who is Connor Kenway?

Connor is the third playable assassin in the Assassin's Creed video game series. He was a Master Assassin of the Colonial Brotherhood during the period of the American Revolutionary War. Being half British and half Mohawk, Connor had a difficult time of fitting into American culture. After spending years fighting off the Templars, the Assassin's mortal enemy, and rebuilding the Colonial Brotherhood, Connor became a central figure in the American Revolution as he helped the Patriot cause with the goal of protecting his people's lands from incursions and preventing the Templars from taking control of the young United States.
